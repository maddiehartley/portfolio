About Me

I'm a graduate journalism student at Northwestern's Medill School, specializing in sports media. I'm a recent graduate of the University of Kansas with a Bachelor of Science in Broadcast Journalism with a minor in Sport Management.

I have a deep passion for highlighting and lifting athlete voices, sharing their experiences beyond production on the field, and taking a deeper dive into the sports that we love.

In addition to my coursework, I've gained a wide range of experience in the sports journalism scene. At The Kansas City Star, I've gained experiences producing features on high school, college and professional athletics, as well as strengthening my reporting skills for college and professional athletics.
